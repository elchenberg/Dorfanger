Dorfanger

Use arrow keys, WASD, TFGH or IJKL to move the animals or select an animal with your mouse/finger and move it to a neighbouring field with another click/touch.

Twitter: twitter.com/elchenberg
Emoji: Noto Emoji, Apache License 2.0
Source: github.com/elchenberg/Dorfanger, MIT License

A small and simple tile-based map with a few emoji animals to move around. Written in HTML, CSS, and Javascript over the course of a weekend with page speed, browser compatibility, and responsive web design in mind.
